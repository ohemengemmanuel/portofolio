Pool Collection

Seven bodies of water,
one living resort

Laguna Azul
1,200m² — 1.4m depth — 30°C

The main social pool — expansive, sunlit, and edged with natural volcanic stone. A gathering place for all guests.

The Plunge
Deep well — 5m depth — 22°C

A cold-shock deep plunge pool with underwater lighting, designed for contrast therapy and breath-work sessions.

Grotto Thermal
Cave pool — 0.9m depth — 40°C

Hidden within a sculpted rock grotto, this hydrotherapy pool features jet benches and mineral-enriched hot water.

Canopy Falls
Waterfall pool — 1.2m — 28°C

Below a 7-metre cascading waterfall shielded by tropical planting — a natural escape from the open sky.

Horizon Edge
Infinity design — 1.5m — 29°C

Our rooftop infinity pool, cantilevered over the Atlantic. At sunset, the water blurs into the ocean horizon.

Toddler Cove
Play zone — 0.4m — 32°C

A purpose-built shallow splash zone with gentle jets and tipping buckets — the youngest explorers' favourite.

FAQ

Everything you
need to know

TIDAL is open 365 days a year. Regular hours are 8am to 10pm. Midnight Swim sessions run every Thursday from 10pm to 1am for members and ticket holders. The Abyssal rooftop restaurant operates Tuesday to Sunday from 6pm.
TIDAL welcomes all ages. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. The Abyss Spiral Slide and Surf Engine have a minimum height requirement of 1.2m. The Grotto Thermal and Horizon Edge infinity pool are restricted to guests 16 and above. Midnight Swim sessions are 18+ only.
Absolutely. Day passes are available for walk-ins and online pre-booking. Adult day passes start at GHS 150, children at GHS 80. Pre-booking online saves you 15% and guarantees entry on busy days. Members enjoy unlimited visits and priority booking at all times.
Your day pass includes access to all main pools, the Abyss Spiral Slide, Drift River, Surf Engine, and Canopy Falls. You'll also get a locker, a fresh towel, and complimentary Wi-Fi. Spa treatments, Abyssal dining, and the Grotto Thermal are priced separately unless you're on a Current or Abyss membership tier.
Yes — we have a 200-space car park on site, free for all guests. Abyss members receive a reserved parking space. We also have a complimentary shuttle running from the nearest Accra Metro station every 30 minutes during opening hours.
Outside food is not permitted in the main pool areas, but you're welcome to bring snacks to the picnic lawn near Drift Café. We have three dining venues on site — from casual poolside bites at The Surface bar to fine dining at Abyssal — so you'll always find something to suit your mood and budget.
