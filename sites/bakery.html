Our Story

Born from flour,
passion & a little
obsession.

Aurore began in a small kitchen with a single recipe — a sourdough loaf that our founder Élise refused to stop perfecting. Sixteen years later, the obsession hasn't faded. It's deepened.

We source our flour from heritage grain mills, use only organic butter in our viennoiseries, and let our doughs ferment on their own schedule — never ours. Real bread takes time, and we respect that.
