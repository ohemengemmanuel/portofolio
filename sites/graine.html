The Atelier

How we work
in the dark.

I
The Leaven

Our starter is nine years old. Fed twice daily, never refrigerated. It is the only living thing we bring to work.

II
The Mix

Flour, water, salt. No shortcuts. Autolyse for sixty minutes before the leaven touches it. Patience is the ingredient.

III
The Cold

Every loaf sleeps in a refrigerator for thirty-six hours minimum. Flavor is time. We will not rush it for anyone.

IV
The Fire

A Dutch oven. 260 °C. Steam trapped for twenty minutes. The crust forms like a verdict — irreversible, defining.

"Good bread does not need to announce itself. It is felt in the weight of the loaf, heard in the sound of the crust, and understood only after the first bite."

— Graine, on bread
