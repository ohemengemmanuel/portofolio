Attractions

Something for Everyone

From heart-pounding slides to gentle kiddie pools — Elinesis has the perfect adventure for every age and personality.

Mega Slides

Soar down our multi-lane speed slides and twisting tube slides for maximum thrills and non-stop laughs.

All Ages (Height 1.2m+)

Wave Pool

Ride the waves in our giant wave pool — perfect for relaxing floats or a fun surf experience right here in Oyarifa.

Family Favourite

Splash Kiddie Zone

A shallow, safe water playground designed for toddlers and little ones — spray jets, mini slides, and bucket dumps galore!

Ages 0–7

Lazy River

Drift along our 200m winding lazy river on inflatable rings — the ultimate way for parents to unwind while kids splash around.

Pure Relaxation

Ocean Bites Dining

Jollof rice, grilled tilapia, fresh juices, snacks and international favorites — fueling your day of fun from morning to sunset.

Food & Drinks

Private Cabanas

Reserve a shaded private cabana with lounge chairs, towel service, and a dedicated waitstaff for a VIP poolside experience.

Why Choose Us

A Paradise Built for Joy

We've thought of everything — so all you have to think about is making memories.

Certified Safe & Clean

Our pools are tested daily and all rides inspected by certified safety engineers. Your family's safety is our top priority.

Family Friendly

From infants to grandparents — every zone is designed with a range of ages in mind. Changing rooms, nursing areas, and accessibility ramps included.

Sunny Year-Round

Oyarifa's warm climate means we're open 365 days. Perfect weather, perfect vibes — any weekend works for a water park day.

Events & Birthday Parties

Celebrate in style! We host birthday parties, school outings, and corporate events with custom packages and dedicated event staff.

Eco-Conscious Park

Our water recycling system and solar panels ensure your fun has the lightest possible footprint. We love Ghana's environment.

Easy Online Booking

Skip the queue! Buy tickets online, choose your preferred date, and walk straight to the fun. Group discounts available.
