Frequently asked in the hallway
What exactly is a "room" here? +
A room is a dedicated workspace inside Casa — think of it as a digital room you own. You can fill it with projects, files, tools, and collaborators. Each room has its own key and its own character.
Can I redecorate my rooms? +
Absolutely. Every room supports full theming — colours, layouts, widgets, and custom domains. Move the furniture. Paint the walls. This is your house.
How many housemates can I invite? +
On the Snack plan, you live solo or with one guest. Dinner invites five, and the Feast keeps the door open to everyone — unlimited housemates, always.
What happens if I want to move out? +
We'll pack your bags for you. Full data export available at any time, in standard formats. No lock-in. We'd rather you stay because you love it, not because it's hard to leave.
Is the house secure at night? +
Locks, alarms, the works. End-to-end encryption, 2FA, SSO, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. The neighbourhood watch is always on duty.
