Our Story

Made with Love,
Served with Joy

Scoops & Co. was born from a simple belief: that the best ice cream should use the best ingredients. We source fresh fruits from local Ghanaian farms, blend them with cream from free-range dairy, and churn every batch by hand in small quantities.

From our first tiny parlour in Osu, we've grown to three beloved locations across Accra — but our commitment to quality, creativity, and community has never changed.

Locally Sourced
Fresh fruits and cream from Ghanaian farms within 100km of our kitchen.
No Artificial Flavours
Free from artificial colours, flavours, and preservatives. Always.
Award Winning
Best Artisan Ice Cream — Ghana Food & Hospitality Awards 2022 & 2023.
